ABOUT FDM & DANIEL Nigeria Limited

FDM & Daniel Nigeria Limited (RC 1045325) is a company focused on providing construction management services, with a specialty in creating value for money invested in projects. Our strength lies in our team of active professionals: qualified Quantity Surveyors with over 15 years of experience in both multinational and indigenous construction companies in Nigeria, technical experts from top-tier firms, and a structural engineer with extensive experience in both contracting and consultancy roles.

We believe our extensive network of local and international contacts, combined with our deep experience in the Nigerian construction industry, makes us highly relevant to our clients.

Vision

As a company, we aim to bring excellence to the indigenous construction market. Our core values focus on ensuring construction economy by optimizing the use of our clients' investments.

Mission

Our mission is to be a light that exposes every amount spent on projects without ambiguity, by breaking the entire process into clearly understood units and ensuring effective control. We are committed to delivering projects within budget.

Our objectives

Towards achieving construction economy, we offer:

Design Value Engineering: Assisting with team coordination for synergy and value optimization.

Planning: Developing and monitoring work schedules, cash flow, and procurement plans.

Contracting: Selecting and negotiating with contractors and managing contract packages.

Reporting: Providing monthly cost reports and performance updates.

Our approach to client services

At FDM & Daniel Nigeria Limited, we ensure our service quality is benchmarked against internationally accepted standards and expertise. This commitment is reflected in our cost management practices and our adherence to quality assurance procedures.

We take the time to understand each client’s specific needs and expectations. Our business principles are built on HONESTY, QUALITY, TIMELY DELIVERY, and COST CONTROL.
